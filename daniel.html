Daniel's Bio

I am an alumni from the University of Central Florida with a BA Degree in Radio/TV Production. My road since has gained me experience in various areas. With a creative passion and desire to use my talents to glorify God and help others has been a constant theme. From owning a video production business and maintaining my business website to becoming an experienced Medical Biller/ Data Analytst to later exploring programming, I have found how I can use my talents to help others.

I have over 9 years combined experience in medical billing and claims processing. In 2014-2015, I worked for a start up computer consultation company, Coopdigity as a Data Analyst where I came back to my earlier interest of website design. In May 2017, my road brought me to a web development coding camp called Coder Camp and since I have been continuing to grow my skills in website development.

With having learned the foundational programming languages, search engine optimization, and social networking, I am ready to put my skills to work for you. Now, I am living my dream and entreprenuerial passion and have become the owner of Webceptional Design Co. I am glad to help you get your business off the ground or bring your website to the next level. My business provides web development and maintenance services.
