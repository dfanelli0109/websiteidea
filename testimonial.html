My Testimony & Road to Success

My Faith

I have made the decision to put my faith in my Savior, Jesus Christ, who paid the price and sacrificed himself as a ransom for those who receive his gift of grace and follow his declaration of being the only way, the truth, and the life to salvation. I have been involved in various ministries and believe in using my business or position to display God's love for them.

My Road Towards Becoming A Web Developer

In 2017, I started my journey towards starting the web development boot camp. My wife was pregnant at the time. I thought that I had enough time to complete the program, however half way through my wife's water broke, my wife was dealing with an infection and forced to give birth and our baby not making it after birth. Due to the series of events that occurred, it made it difficult to continue but I did move forward. One July 22, 2018, our son, Elijah was born. Through our journey, I saw one aspect to the issue of how valuable life is and it became my priority above all else, as web development took a backseat.

On July 22, 2018, our long awaited baby, Elijah Daniel Fanelli was born. It was a blessing to many prayers over an extended period of time. It allowed the previous chapter to close and for my quest to move forward with web development to come center stage.
